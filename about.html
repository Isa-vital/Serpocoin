About Serpo Coin

A Cosmic Legend Reimagined for the Blockchain Era

The Legend of Project Serpo

Serpo Coin draws inspiration from one of humanity's greatest mysteries — Project Serpo, the alleged 1960s exchange program between Earth and beings from the Zeta Reticuli star system.

According to declassified documents and whistleblower testimonies, twelve brave astronauts embarked on a journey to planet Serpo, where they lived among an advanced alien civilization for over a decade.

This legendary tale of interstellar cooperation, knowledge exchange, and cosmic unity forms the philosophical foundation of our Web3 project.

Reimagined as a Web3 Civilization

Serpo Coin transforms this cosmic legend into a living digital civilization — where community, technology, and imagination unite.

Just as the original Project Serpo represented an exchange of knowledge between two civilizations, Serpo Coin creates a decentralized ecosystem where members from around the world collaborate, trade, and build together.
